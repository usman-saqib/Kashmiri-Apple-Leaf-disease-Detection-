Diseases We Detect

Comprehensive detection for common apple leaf diseases

🍎

Apple Rot

🔴 Symptoms: Brown spots, decaying tissue, foul smell

💊 Treatment: Apply fungicide, remove infected leaves

🛡️ Prevention: Proper spacing, avoid overhead watering

⚠️ High Severity
🍏

Healthy Leaf

✅ Symptoms: Green color, no spots, normal texture

💚 Treatment: No treatment needed

🌱 Prevention: Regular monitoring, proper care

✅ No Issue
🍂

Leaf Blotch

🟡 Symptoms: Irregular brown/yellow patches, leaf curling

💊 Treatment: Apply copper-based fungicide

🛡️ Prevention: Good air circulation, avoid wet leaves

📊 Medium Severity
🍁

Apple Scab

⚫ Symptoms: Olive-green to black spots, cracked leaves

💊 Treatment: Sulfur spray, remove infected leaves

🛡️ Prevention: Resistant varieties, proper sanitation

⚠️ High Severity
